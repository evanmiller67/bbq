Brock / Marcum Wedding BBQ Deliciousness
========================================

## Noms to make
Sandwichs for ~70.  
Equates to ~45 lbs uncooked.  

- Pork (30 lbs) 
- Chicken (15 lbs) 
- Buns (70 ct) 
- BBQ Sauce (2 lg bottles) 
- Condiments 
    - Onion (1-2) 
    - Pickle (1 jar) 
    - Cole Slaw (1-2 lb) 
- Sides
    - Baked Beans
    - Potato Salad
    - Pasta Salad

## Dish it up
#### Serving pieces
- Foil half pans with lids
- Serving tongs

#### Individual place settings
- Paper plates


## Stoke teh firez
- Charcoal
- Wood chips/chunks
- Spices
