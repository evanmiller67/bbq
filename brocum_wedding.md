## Noms to make
Sandwichs for ~70.  
Equates to ~45 lbs uncooked.

- Chicken (15 lbs) 
- Buns (70 ct) 
- BBQ Sauce (2 lg bottles) 
- Condiments 
    - Onion (1-2) 
    - Pickle (1 jar) 
    - Cole Slaw (1-2 lb)
